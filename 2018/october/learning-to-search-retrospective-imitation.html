Imitation Learning

Initially, learn a policy based off of supervised learning. Use a "human expert" to demonstrate some examples of desired actions.

This is problematic because the testing data will eventually diverge from the training set. DAgger (Data Aggregation) will correct the original policy by playing some reinforcement learning sessions and then ask the human to label some of them.

How can we do this with less expert data?

Retrospective Imitation

Idea: policy practices and reflects on its mistakes
